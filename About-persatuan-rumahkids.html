ABOUT RUMAH K.I.D.S

Persatuan Rumah K.I.D.S. has been operating shelter homes for the orphaned, the abused, abandoned, and neglected children since 1991. Presently we are caring for 42 children from 1 year old to 18 years of age in 4 homes. More than 300 children have grown up in our shelter homes since 1991.


We are registered with the Registrar of Societies Welfare Department, Majlis Perbandaran Klang and other related government departments to ensure the legal operation of our Home. Each year our accounts are audited to assure you of our accountability.
